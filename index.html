Who We Are

Built From
150 Years
of Legacy

Etairos was formed through the merger of Gorham/Schaffler and Mills Wilson George — the two oldest rep firms in Memphis — then joined by Delta Thermal Solutions, the longest-standing HVAC rep in the Jackson, MS market.

Etairos is Biblical Greek for "trusted partner." Our logo, the fire-breathing polar bear, represents endurance, tenacity, and protection. That same character defines how we approach every project: we own the outcome from budgeting and design selection through installation, start-up, and ongoing maintenance.

"We customize high-performance, economical, sustainable mechanical systems for designers, builders and owners."
